How to Apply for RTI Online in Inderavelly (2025) – Step-by-Step Filing Guide

Residents of Inderavelly mandal in Adilabad district can access government information under the RTI Act, 2005, through online and offline methods. This guide covers queries on land records, local governance, public works, and more, ensuring a smooth filing process as of August 2025.

Key Officials for RTI in Inderavelly

  • Mandal PIO: Tahsildar, Inderavelly Mandal Office (Contact via Adilabad district directory for current details).
  • Appellate Authority: Revenue Divisional Officer (RDO), Adilabad Division (Mobile: 9492422444 | Landline: 08732-226557).
  • District Level: Refer to Adilabad Collectorate for escalated queries (Administrative Officer: Mobile 9491095356 | Landline: 08732-231300).

Address applications to the local Tahsildar for mandal-specific issues to ensure quick responses.

Step-by-Step Process to File RTI in Inderavelly

  1. Draft Your Application: Start with “Application under RTI Act 2005.” Ask clear questions about public records in English, Hindi, or Telugu. Include your name, address, contact, date, and citizenship declaration.
  2. Address It Correctly: Direct to the Inderavelly Tahsildar or relevant department PIO. Use the district directory if unsure.
  3. Pay the Fee: Rs. 10 via court fee stamp, DD, IPO, or online payment. Add Rs. 2 per page for document copies.
  4. Submission Options:
    • Online: Via Telangana RTI Portal. Choose Adilabad district, Inderavelly mandal, upload docs, and pay digitally.
    • Offline: Submit in person or by post to the Mandal Office.
  5. Track Your RTI: Note the reference number and keep copies for follow-up.
  6. Response Time: Expect a reply within 30 days (48 hours for urgent cases involving life/liberty).
  7. Appeals Process:
    • First Appeal: To the RDO within 30 days if no response.
    • Second Appeal: To Telangana State Information Commission.

Common RTI Topics in Inderavelly

  • Land surveys and revenue details (Tahsildar Office).
  • Local infrastructure like roads and water (Mandal Development Officer).
  • Education and health schemes (District-level officers via mandal).
  • MGNREGA works, welfare funds, and village development.

Keep requests specific. Check the Adilabad Contact Directory for contacts.

Tips for Successful RTI Filing

  • Limit to one subject per application for faster processing.
  • File separately for multi-department queries.
  • Allow 5 extra days if transferred to Adilabad district level.
  • Use official portals for High Court-related RTIs.
  • For assisted filing, try platforms like File My RTI for digital support.
